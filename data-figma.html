Data Visualization

Redrawing Adobe XD Charts in Figma

I am excited to share some data chart templates that I have made over time in AdobeXd and am currently redrawing in Figma. In designing charts, I always stress the importants of having a grid and readable labels. Often charts are designed to capture the attention of the audience however when these charts need close interpretation and analysis, clear lableing and descriptions are extremly important.

Dashboards can take a long time to both design and build, which is why pins are very useful. I will present each chart individually as a pin which can be combined with other pins to make a custom dashboard.

This section will be updated regularly and soon contain 100 charts.

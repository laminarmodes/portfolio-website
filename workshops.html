Design Sprints

Developing a strategy and designing products can be messy and I found workshops to be a great way to bring structure, enjoyment and momentum to the process.

Right before COVID I learned how to run Design Sprints from AJ&Smart and as soon as I fed my projects into this Sprint framework, they immediately gained a new momentum. Each sprint produced a high fidelity tested prototype that we could then break down into a project plan and backlog.
